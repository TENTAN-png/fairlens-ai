Build FairLens AI as a modern, enterprise-grade Responsible AI Governance Platform focused on fairness, transparency, accountability, and compliance.

IMPORTANT:

The platform must be designed for non-technical users such as HR managers, recruiters, compliance officers, business leaders, educators, government officials, and policy makers.

Avoid displaying technical terms such as:

* Disparate Impact
* Statistical Parity Difference
* Equalized Odds
* Counterfactual Fairness
* SHAP
* LIME
* Bias Mitigation Algorithms
* Fairness Constraints

Instead, use simple, user-friendly language.

Examples:

Disparate Impact → Fairness Health Score

Statistical Parity Difference → Fairness Gap

Counterfactual Fairness → Decision Consistency Check

Bias Detection → Fairness Review

Mitigation → Improvement Suggestions

Protected Attributes → Personal Characteristics

SHAP Feature Importance → Key Decision Drivers

Root Cause Analysis → Why This Happened

Every feature must include a plain-English explanation understandable by someone with no AI background.
